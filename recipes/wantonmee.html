Wanton Mee

This is a popular noodle dish in Asia and many Chinese Hong Kong-style restaurants in the United States.

There are two types of this popular dish: dry or with soup. This page will contain the dry version.

In the dry version, the wanton noodles are tossed with a dark sauce and topped with boiled wantons or fried wantons, char siu and some green leafy choy sum.

The noodles are great with pickled chillies. They are an essential condiment to this dish and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Fresh egg noodles
  • Wanton skin
  • Pork for wanton filling
  • Char siu
  • Garlic oil
  • Choy sum
  • Mushrooms
  • Pickled chillies

Steps

  1. Prepare pickled green chillies
  2. Prepare wanton filling
  3. Fold the wantons
  4. Prepare garlic oil
  5. Prepare mushroom sauce
  6. Blanch the vegetables
  7. Cook the wantons
  8. Prepare the noodles
  9. Garnish and serve!
